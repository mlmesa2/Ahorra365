# PRIVACY POLICY - Savings365
Last update: January 2026

### 1. Introduction
Welcome to Savings365. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information when you use our mobile application Savings365 ("the App", "we", "our").

In summary: Savings365 does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All your information is stored locally on your device.

### 2. Information We DO NOT Collect
Savings365 is designed with privacy as a priority. We DO NOT collect, store on external servers, or share:

❌ Personally identifiable information (name, email, phone)
❌ Financial information (bank accounts, credit cards)
❌ Location data
❌ App usage data
❌ Device identifiers
❌ Analytics or telemetry data
❌ Cookies or tracking technologies

### 3. Information Stored Locally
All information generated by the App is stored exclusively on your device through:

#### 3.1 Local Database (Room)
Generated daily challenges (amounts and dates)
Completion status of each challenge
Unlocked achievements
Progress history
#### 3.2 User Preferences (DataStore)
Notification settings
Preferred time for reminders
Selected currency symbol
Auto-reset preference
Streak statistics
Important: This information NEVER leaves your device. We have no access to it and it is not synced with any server.

### 4. Application Permissions
The App requests the following Android permissions:

#### 4.1 Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS)
Purpose: Send daily challenge reminders
Use: Only used for local notifications generated on your device
Optional: You can disable notifications anytime from the App settings
#### 4.2 Exact Alarms (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM, USE_EXACT_ALARM)
Purpose: Schedule daily notifications at the exact time you configure
Use: Allows notifications to arrive punctually
Optional: Only used if notifications are enabled
Note: The App does NOT require internet connection and does NOT request network permissions.

### 5. Third-Party Services
Ahorro365 does NOT use any third-party services, including:

❌ Advertising services
❌ Social networks
❌ Analytics services
### 6. Data Security
Although we don't collect data, we implement the following security measures:

✅ Encrypted local storage (Android Keystore)
✅ No network data transmission
✅ No remote access to information
✅ Code following Android security best practices
### 7. User Rights
As a user of Savings365, you have full control over your data:

#### 7.1 Access
All your data is on your device and accessible at any time
#### 7.2 Modification
You can modify any setting from the App
#### 7.3 Deletion
Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all data
You can reset the challenge from settings to erase current progress
#### 7.4 Portability
Your data is on your device and not locked in any service

### 8. Children's Privacy
Savings365 is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect information from minors or any other users. Parents can rest assured knowing that:

There is no personal data collection
There is no communication with external servers
There is no advertising or inappropriate content
